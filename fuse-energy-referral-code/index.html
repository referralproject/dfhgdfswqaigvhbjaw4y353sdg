What to check before switching to Fuse Energy

If you are already planning to move supplier, using a referral code makes sense. But the supplier still needs to fit your home and tariff needs.

  • Unit rate: compare the current rate in your region against your current supplier.
  • Standing charge: a cheaper headline tariff can still be weaker if the daily standing charge is higher.
  • Tariff type: check whether you are joining a variable or fixed tariff and whether any exit fees apply.
  • Payment setup: make sure the direct debit and billing arrangement works for you.
  • Referral campaign status: confirm the referral promotion is still live before you complete your switch.
